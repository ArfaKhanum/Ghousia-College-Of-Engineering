About Ghousia College Of Engineering

Ghousia College Of Engineering was established in the year 1980. The College is preparing the students of Karnataka and other states of India to establish themselves in the competiting market. It is managed by Ghousia Industrial & Engineering Trust, Bangalore
The products that we supply at SMP Meets the best safety and quality standards. We are being audited regularly by officials to ensure that the highest safety and quality criteria were met.

The College is AICTE, New Delhi approved and is affiliated to the Visveswaraiah Technological University, Belgaum. The location of the college is at Ramanagaram on Mysore-Bangalore highway which is 45 Kms away from Bangalore. The college has celebrated its Silver Jubilee in 2003-04. Due to its dedication in the field of education, GCE has been recognized as one of the good Engineering Colleges in Karnataka State. The College offers UG engineering courses in six disciplines, PG engineering courses in four streams, and Research Courses in various departments.
